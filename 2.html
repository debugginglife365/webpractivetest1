Download and install Music Manager On your computer, go to the Music Manager download page. Click Download Music Manager. Follow the installation directions. Set up Music Manager Open Music Manager from your Applications folder (Mac) or from the Start menu (Windows). Sign in to your Google account. Select the location of your music collection.
