You can only upload one type of music collection at a time (iTunes, or Windows Media Player, or folders). To change this:

Select the Music Manager icon Music Manager.

You'll find this at the bottom right of your screen (Windows) or the menu bar in the upper right corner (Mac).

Select Preferences > Advanced > Change. Select the location of your music collection. Follow the on-screen instructions.
